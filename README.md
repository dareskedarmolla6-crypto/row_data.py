# FSE-Core: Intelligent Crypto Trading System

FSE-Core is an advanced automated crypto trading framework designed for precision, risk management, and market intelligence.

## Architecture Modules
- **brain/**: AI logic and predictive analysis.
- **risk/**: Capital protection and risk management (manager.py).
- **strategy/**: SMC and trend-following algorithms.
- **execution/**: API-based order management.
- **portfolio/**: Asset tracking and performance monitoring.
- **data/**: Historical datasets and market intelligence.
- **cloud/**: Deployment and scaling configurations.
- **monitoring/**: System health and logging.
- **config/**: Security and system secrets.
- **docs/**: Technical documentation.
- **testing/**: Backtesting engine.
- **utils/**: Shared helper functions.

## Goal
To automate and professionalize crypto trading through structured, modular development.
